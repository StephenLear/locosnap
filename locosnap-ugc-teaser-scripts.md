## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Avatar / Creator Profile
- Age: 25–40
- Vibe: Genuine railfan — slightly worn jacket, maybe a hi-vis vest, hat. Not overly polished.
- Energy: Enthusiastic but not salesy. Talks like they're texting a mate a voice note.
- Background: Real railway environments — stations, platforms, heritage lines, depots. Authentic > aesthetic.

### Format
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (Instagram Reels + X vertical)
- Length: 30–40 seconds each
- Captions: Always on (many watch on mute)
- Music: Low, ambient — don't compete with speech. Optional subtle train ambience (wheel rumble, station PA).

### Hook Rate Target
Scripts 1–4 are teasers: optimise for **saves + follows**, not link clicks.
Script 5 (launch day): optimise for **link taps + downloads**.
