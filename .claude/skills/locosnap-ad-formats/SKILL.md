# LocoSnap Ad Format Menu

A library of 100 viral short-form formats, sorted by whether they survive LocoSnap's hard-won rules. This is an idea menu, NOT a green-light list — every pick still runs through `feedback_ad_build_pipeline` and the gate below.

## THE GATE (non-negotiable — read first)

**Every ad opens train-in-frame-1.** Formats whose native form opens on a face, a text/stat card, a talking head, or a skit setup DIE at 0:01. Proven flops, all same signature (~3.1s avg watch):
- BR 140 history-lecture cold open — 4.7% full-watch
- PL "POLSKA PRZEJMUJE" text-stat-card open — 8.8% full-watch

Validated shapes (what actually retains for us):
- Multi-beat hero-concat (beat-sync montage) — **25.2% ceiling, our best**
- Single-hero — SU46 ~23%
- Tier-debate / feature tier list — EN57 20.7% (PL), ICE 1 15.5% (DE)
- Scan-day POV, scan→RARE-card reveal, comment-reply (loco-first) — in rotation

Other standing rules: no emojis ever; DE is the default market (PL #2, train-first only — see `feedback_de_ad_default_and_uk_pivot_cost`); paywall/price objections answered mechanism/identity-first, never apologetic (`feedback_paywall_reframe_no_apology`); positioning is collection-is-the-moat, hobby/Sammelkarten, NOT productivity (`project_competitive_positioning`); ground every pick in recent data first (`feedback_ad_planning_grounded_in_data`).

## Bucket C — Traps for us (avoid, or rebuild from scratch)

Native form is non-loco-first (face/text/skit open = the flop signature) or off-brand for a hobby collection app. These are exactly the shapes that died.

- **Non-loco-first opens (0:01 death):** 2 Fake FaceTime · 13 Fake Text Messages · 38 Fake News Report · 18 Green Screen Breakdown · 27 Street Interviews · 61 Pointing Text Trend · 70 Lip-Sync Metaphor
- **Lecture/story openers (BR 140 pattern):** 31 Founder Story · 58 Storytime · 69 I Quit My Job · 46 Review Vlog
- **Vlog / face-forward (no loco frame 1):** 8 Day in the Life · 23 Micro Vlog · 52 Productivity Vlog · 56 Night Routine · 34 GRWM · 11 Workspace Routine · 49 Clean My Phone
- **Skits (off-brand):** 12 Relatable Skit · 21 My Toxic Trait · 37 Comedy Skit · 53 My Boss Doesn't Know · 54 Couple Skit

## How to use this skill

1. Read the recent handover + `tiktok_stats.md` first — what just ran, what's the market, what's the audience trend.
2. Pick from **Bucket A** (safest) or **Bucket B** (only with the loco-first rework). Never ship a Bucket C format without rebuilding it loco-first — and if you do rework one, log the result so we learn whether the rework holds.
3. Run the chosen format through `feedback_ad_build_pipeline` (frame-sample → place text → frame-check) and confirm the loco-first cold open.
4. Log every build + read in `tiktok_stats.md`; promote a Bucket B format to Bucket A here once it clears the 15% full-watch band in our own data.
