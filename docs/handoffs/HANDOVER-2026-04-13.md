## What Worked
- **"NUR NOCH 4" as opening hook** — more impactful scroll-stopper than starting with the year (SEIT 1974). The contrast 227 → 4 delivers the story instantly.
- **Hamburg loco footage** — 1080x1920 native portrait, fleet number clearly readable, dynamic approach shot. Best single-clip source of the three available options.
- **Larger text sizes** — user requested bigger text. DE: 110/120/130/150px tiers. Cleaner on mobile than the previous 110px default.
- **Full render then extract frames for subtitle verification** — not using -ss before -i for subtitle timing checks. -ss resets the ASS clock to 0 causing all frames to show the t=0 subtitle.

## What Didn't Work (and Fixes)
- **Watermark drawbox iterations** — needed 4 attempts to cover @TrainspottingDE193. drawbox at y=1200, y=1210, y=1160 all still showed watermark. Fix: full-width drawbox at y=1160 h=120 definitively covered it. Root cause: watermark in source at y≈1820/1920 = y≈1213 after scaling, but sits right at boundary of conservative box estimates.
- **-ss before -i for subtitle test frames** — all test frames showed "NUR NOCH 4" regardless of timestamp because ASS clock resets to 0 on seek. Fix: render full video first, then extract frames from output.
- **crop + pad approach for watermark** — tried crop=720:1200:0:0,pad=720:1280:0:0 but watermark persisted at boundary. drawbox was the reliable fix.

## Key Decisions
- **"End of era" series confirmed** — BR101, BR218, BR111 form a deliberate thematic series for the German market. Series identity = account identity for German rail fans.
- **NUR NOCH 4 as hook, not SEIT 1974** — scarcity (4 remaining) is a stronger scroll-stopper than longevity (since 1974). The year moves to position 3 as context, not hook.
- **"NUR NOCH 4" is defensible** — number refers to locos in regular Planbetrieb on RB 6. If challenged, reply "4 im regelmäßigen Planbetrieb auf der RB 6 München-Garmisch" is accurate per eisenbahn.de.
- **227 not used in video text** — kept for caption only. VON 227 / FROM 227 does the contrast work without needing the full number on screen.
- **FROM 1974 not SINCE 1974 in EN** — "SINCE 1974" = 10 chars, exceeds 110px limit (~9 chars at 720px). "FROM 1974" = 9 chars, fits safely.
- **DE only content strategy holds** — BR218 stats confirmed: DE TikTok 63.9% Germany, DE Instagram 80.5% Germany. UK 9.5% on EN TikTok still small. Continue DE-first.

## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- **Subtitle timing with -ss: always render first** — using -ss as input flag resets ASS clock. For any subtitle verification, render the full video then extract frames. This is the only reliable approach.
- **Watermark removal: be aggressive early** — start with a full-width drawbox at least 120px from the bottom rather than trying to minimally cover just the watermark. Saves multiple rebuild cycles.
- **IC 2012/2013 = named services not years** — TikTok commenter asking about "die am 2012 und 2013" meant the IC Allgäu services numbered 2012/2013, not years or fleet numbers. BR218 double traction on these ends June 2026 when ICE-L takes over.
