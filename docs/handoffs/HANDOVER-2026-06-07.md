## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- **Confirm chart scope before logging** — App Store Connect "Territories" bar defaults to ALL apps in the account (US in the top ranks = the tell it's unfiltered); the Anthropic console here IS LocoSnap-only (separate dedicated account). Don't assume.
- **Use `public.spots` for scan volume**, never `profiles.daily_scans_used` (Pro scans don't increment it; counter < spots).
- **AI cost driver = vision-prompt cache WARMTH (traffic concentration), not raw volume/novelty.** Clustered scans cheap (Jun 4: $0.052 despite 87% novel); dispersed many-user days dear (Jun 1: $0.228). Jun 1 high cost is the caching-cold worst case, not an anomaly.
- **TikTok now caps captions at 5 hashtags.**
- **Don't make absolute retirement claims** for slipping/role-dependent classes — the 218 caption "verschwindet vom Netz" is too strong; use "letzter planmäßiger Einsatz." (Same family as the SU46 "20 vs 6" trap.)
- **On "your reply is AI" jabs** — short human-in-the-loop line ("z wami pisze człowiek"); never overclaim 100% human.
