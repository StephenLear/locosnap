## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- **"The payment is pending." is NOT a bug** — RC `PAYMENT_PENDING_ERROR`, expected Google Play deferred payment, self-resolving. Only act if volume spikes.
- **Never use a bare "only N left" scarcity hook** for a class with a changing fleet (SU46) — it becomes a contradiction magnet (the old "~20 left" caption vs the now-preserved count = Qwety's "20 vs 6" catch). SU46 caption fleet-counts already banned since the 06-03 haul note; this is the proof of why.
- **On "your replies are ChatGPT" accusations** — deflect with the human-in-the-loop truth ("a real person reads + posts every reply"); never overclaim "100% human" (false, and they'd catch it). Recommended cheeky line reframes AI as the product's strength: "Apka używa AI do rozpoznawania pociągów — z wami gada człowiek."
- **On skill/"I do it myself free" objections** — do NOT end with "it's free whenever you want"; it reopens the price fight. End on personal choice. Never say "not for pro spotters" (we have pro evangelists). Reuse "łup" (catch) to tie replies to the haul-ad theme.
- **More comments drive the algorithm** (Stephen) — reply to every pile-on individually, vary wording, hold the collection-moat posture.
- **Hostile threads can flip to warm rapport** — confident no-apology reframe → take any softening at face value → light human/witty close (NO re-selling at the warm moment, it turns it back into a pitch) → then STOP. The lezba "you're ChatGPT" → bilingual-banter close is the canonical arc (`tiktok_stats`). Our replies stay emoji-free even when theirs aren't.
