## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- The maxSpeed mismatch rule (`>20% → use AI`) was introduced as a safety net for stale Wikidata but backfired badly on ICE 3 — Wikidata was right, AI was wrong. When adding override logic, always consider which source is more likely to be wrong.
- Temperature 0 should be the default on all AI calls in this app. There is no use case where randomness in train identification or spec generation is desirable.
- Do not state App Store Connect capabilities without being certain. "Metadata-only changes don't need a new binary" is not always true — caveat it.
- German rail enthusiasts are the most technically precise commenters you will encounter. Nero went from critic to potential tester in four exchanges. Engage them properly and they become advocates.
- Follower gender split (57% female) vs Frankfurt video viewer split (59% male) shows two distinct audiences: casual discovery followers vs technical rail enthusiasts. Content strategy should serve both.
