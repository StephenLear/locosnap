## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- Always frame-check every second of a recording before using in video — gallery picker exposes personal photos even briefly
- Render free/hobby tier cold-starts after inactivity — first request after a deploy can timeout and show "Could not identify a train"
- The in-memory trainCache resets on every Render deploy — cached results are ephemeral
- TikTok drop-off at 0:01 = hook problem not distribution problem — algorithm is working (89.5% FYP), content isn't stopping the scroll
- When reviewing screen recordings for ads: check 0s, 1s, and every 3s through the full duration before committing to use
