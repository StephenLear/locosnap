## Key Decisions
- **Email over WhatsApp for APK links**: More reliable, no warning screens, opens directly in browser
- **Reuse phone screenshots for tablet slots**: Google accepts this, unblocks store listing submission
- **pingRedis on every health check**: Minimal overhead, solves Upstash inactivity without separate cron job

## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- Upstash free tier archives after inactivity — `getRedisStatus()` alone is NOT sufficient to keep it alive, must send a real command
- Google Play tablet screenshot fields (7-inch and 10-inch) are marked required with `*` — easy to miss when scrolling
