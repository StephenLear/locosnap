- **Supabase OTP expired (403)**: Confirmation email link expired during testing → Sign up again for fresh link. Long-term: increase OTP expiry in Supabase dashboard to 86400s
- **"Could not connect to LocoSnap servers"**: Render free tier server sleeping (cold start) → Hit `/api/health` to wake it, then retry. Long-term: paid Render tier ($7/mo) or cron-job.org keep-alive ping
- **Google Play opt-in "App not available"**: Google account on phone didn't match email added in Play Console → Switch Play Store account on device to match

## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- Android sends non-standard MIME types (`image/jpg`, `image/heic`) — always normalise before upload and validate server-side
- Play Store internal testing opt-in requires the tester's **exact Google account email** — the most common failure is email mismatch between Play Console and the account on the device
- Render free tier spins down after inactivity — expect cold starts of 30–60s for the first request. Warn users or add a keep-alive
- Landing page is **not in git** — it's a single file at `/Users/StephenLear/Projects/locosnap-landing/index.html`, deployed directly via Vercel CLI. Changes are not version-controlled
- Screenshot search: check `frontend/assets/screenshots/` first before scanning Desktop
