## What Worked
- **"NUR NOCH 4" as opening hook** — scarcity hook (4 remaining) stronger than trivia comparison hook (ICE 1 "faster than ICE 4"). ICE 1 stats confirm this.
- **Full-width drawbox for watermark removal** — `drawbox=x=0:y=1160:w=720:h=120:color=black:t=fill` definitively removes bottom watermarks without iteration.
- **Render-then-extract for subtitle timing** — render full video then extract frames is the only reliable method. `-ss` before `-i` resets ASS clock to t=0.
- **DE-first content strategy confirmed** — BR101 83.9% Germany, BR218 DE 63.9% Germany. EN audience still small. Series identity forming around German "end of era" locos.

## What Didn't Work (and Fixes)
- **ICE 1 trivia-comparison format** — "fährt noch immer schneller als der neue ICE 4" did not hook the algorithm or audience. Germany only 21.1%, lowest of any German video. Fix: stay with "end of era" / scarcity / year-specific hooks for German content.
- **Docs not updated at session end** — architecture and changelog were not current after the v1.0.18/v1.0.19 session. Fixed this session + added mandatory rule to CLAUDE.md.
- **ET22 speed fix treated as outstanding** — was already fixed in a prior session. Removed from backlog.

## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- **Trivia-comparison hooks underperform vs scarcity/end-of-era** — ICE 1 at 164 views vs BR101 at 1,500 views. The emotional hook matters more than factual interest.
- **Algorithm distribution tells the story** — For You 80.7% on ICE 1 vs 94-96% on other videos. Low FY% = algorithm did not push. This is the number to watch first when evaluating a video.
- **Frontend/backend paywall sync is critical** — backend at 10/month, frontend at 3 lifetime after v1.0.19 ships. Push backend out of sync before = 31 Sentry events, 19 users with confusing 429s.
- **Subtitle `-ss` before `-i` resets ASS clock** — all frames show t=0 subtitle. Always render full video first.
- **Watermark removal: start aggressive** — full-width strip 120px from bottom as default. Saves 3-4 rebuild cycles.
- **v1.0.18 is live, not v1.0.17** — confirmed by user at session start. Architecture doc now reflects this.
