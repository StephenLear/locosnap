## Lessons Learned & Gotchas

- **Read before recommending ad themes.** TikTok follower share and App Store download trend are NOT the same signal. UK content reaches UK audience on TikTok but doesn't convert on App Store. Default DE unless user explicitly cleared a pivot. Saved as `feedback_de_ad_default_and_uk_pivot_cost.md` with "read memory first" steps.
- **ARCHITECTURE.md §19 "10–11 char safe limit at 110px" is letter-width-dependent.** Words with all-wide letters (BUNDESWEHR, DIESE WOCHE) overflow earlier. Rule nuance: for strings where every char is a wide letter (W/H/M/N/U/D/B/E), the safe limit is 8–9 chars at 110px. For mixed strings with narrow chars (digits, I, spaces), the 10–11 rule still holds. Logged in `project_status.md` Known Quirks.
- **Specs-layer hallucinations are not caught by the narrative-layer check.** BR 232 Fun Facts text mentioned Voroshilovgrad correctly; the structured Builder field still said Siemens. Moral: always on-device-scan before publishing an ad that foregrounds specs. The narrative layer is an unreliable proxy for the structured layer.
- **Political ads can be framed as historical observation.** The Ukraine-built-loco-hauls-German-tanks story is inherently geopolitical, but three present-tense clauses with no adjectives ("1973 in Luhansk gebaut. Heute zieht sie Bundeswehr-Panzer. Morgen verschwindet sie") lets the irony land without the account taking a position. Viable framing for future content where the story is necessarily topical.
- **"Reflect → skill" outputs are retrievable via handover grep.** User asked about a saved end-screen element — the trail through handovers revealed the `docs/assets/locosnap_end_screen.mp4` canonical file, created 2026-04-09, skill-rule-3 ("check `docs/assets/` before rebuilding"). Confirms the reflect→skill→handover→future-session loop works as designed.
- **Android developer verification deadline (Sept 2026) is far off but worth noting now.** Google auto-registered the Play Store signing key. EAS preview APK key may or may not be the same — open tech-debt question. Not urgent; resolve before Sept 2026 or stop APK distribution.
- **EAS build credits at 100% for April, pay-as-you-go rates from here.** User approval now explicit per build per `feedback_build_approval.md`.
