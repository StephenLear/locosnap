## Key Decisions
- **Free tier 3 (eyes-open reversal)** — 3 drew backlash in April; now mitigated by the €1 intro + annual-first paywall. Backend gate staged at 6 to avoid server-erroring old-build users.
- **Hard paywall rejected for a tuned credit model**; **annual-first** (paywall already is); **€1 stays on monthly** (a 1-month intro can't attach to a yearly plan; annual pushed by the discount instead).
- **Annual cut across the board** (user call) — all decreases, goodwill, makes annual attractive everywhere.
- **Play win-back + monthly→annual upsell = a tested 1.0.37 payment build, NOT rushed** — payment-flow code, can't unit-test a purchase.
- **No direct email to active subs** — used store-native win-back + a targeted email only to the *recent paying prepaid* lapsers (defensible CRM, unsubscribe included).

## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- **Both Play win-backs (new annual + existing monthly) are DORMANT** — the app only calls `purchasePackage` (no offer/subscriptionOption selection). The existing monthly one has likely never fired. Fix = the 1.0.37 app feature.
- **Cumulative scan-gate caveat** — pre-signup (3) + post-signup `daily_scans_used` (3) are independent, so a scan-first-then-signup user can reach up to 6. Accepted for v1; seeding deferred.
- **Play monthly ≠ Apple monthly** — Google auto-converted it to ~€4.19 all along; never lower it to match Apple (only sheds revenue).
- **Sequence store-config BEFORE a build when a UI feature depends on it** (the SAVE% badge needs live prices). See `lessons.md`.
