## What Worked
- **"NUR NOCH 4" as opening hook** — scarcity (4 remaining) is a stronger scroll-stopper than longevity (SEIT 1974). Sequence delivers contrast 227 → 4 instantly.
- **Full-width drawbox for watermark removal** — `drawbox=x=0:y=1160:w=720:h=120:color=black:t=fill` definitively covered @TrainspottingDE193. No iteration needed when starting aggressive.
- **Render-then-extract for subtitle verification** — rendering full video then extracting frames gives accurate subtitle timing. -ss before -i resets ASS clock to t=0.
- **DE-first content strategy confirmed** — BR218 DE: 63.9% Germany on TikTok, 80.5% Germany on Instagram. EN audience still small (UK 9.5%). Series identity forming around German "end of era" locos.

## What Didn't Work (and Fixes)
- **Watermark drawbox iterations** — 4 attempts before finding the correct y value. Root cause: watermark at y≈1820/1920 in source scales to y≈1213 at 720px width, sits right at boundary of conservative estimates. Fix: always use full-width strip from y=1160 downward.
- **-ss before -i for subtitle test frames** — all frames showed t=0 subtitle ("NUR NOCH 4") regardless of requested timestamp. Fix: render full video first, extract frames from output file only.
- **"FROM 1974" not "SINCE 1974" in EN** — "SINCE 1974" = 10 chars, exceeds 110px Arial Black limit at 720px (~9 chars). "FROM 1974" = 9 chars, fits safely. Test frame confirmed.
- **"Launch day" framing error** — called 11 downloads "launch day." App has been on App Store for months. Corrected in memory.
- **v1.0.18 "waiting for review" reversion** — kept reverting to this framing after user confirmed v1.0.18 was live at session start. Fixed in project_status.md.

## Lessons Learned & Gotchas
- **Subtitle -ss timing: always render first** — -ss as input flag resets ASS clock. For any subtitle verification, render full video then extract frames. Never shortcut this.
- **Watermark removal: start aggressive** — full-width strip 120px from bottom as default. Saves multiple rebuild cycles. Never try to minimally cover the watermark first.
- **IC 2012/2013 = service numbers not years** — common source of confusion in comments. IC Allgäu services numbered 2012 and 2013, BR218 traction from Ulm ends June 2026.
- **Frontend/backend paywall sync is critical** — backend at 10/month, frontend at 3 lifetime after v1.0.19 ships. These MUST go live together. Pushed backend out of sync before = 31 Sentry events, 19 users with confusing 429s.
- **EAS build credits** — were at 95%+ for April as of 2026-04-12. All remaining builds are pay-as-you-go. Run pre-build-git-verification before every build.
